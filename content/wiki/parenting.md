Northwestern provides a number of resources for parents that CS PhD students have found helpful, as well as several that students have been unaware of.
Below is several of the resources that NU can offer parents:

- Parenting advice and guidance consultations [1]
- Student advisory groups (two listed on [1])
- Childcare financial support grants [2, 3]
- Information about childcare centers and resources [2]
- Family leaves of absence [2, 3]
