Northwestern offers [graduate
housing](https://www.northwestern.edu/living/graduate-housing/), but off-campus
housing can be cheaper and nicer to live in.

## Resources for Finding Off-Campus Housing

- [Northwestern Off-Campus Housing](https://offcampushousing.northwestern.edu/)
- [Places4Students](https://www.places4students.com/northwestern)
- [Zillow](https://zillow.com)
- [Apartments.com](https://apartments.com)
- [Facebook](https://facebook.com): Be careful of scams. The [Northwestern
  Apartments and Housing](https://www.facebook.com/groups/302938398232484) group
  has worked well for some graduate students in the past.

## Chicago or Evanston?

As a general rule, the closer an apartment is to campus (either the Evanston or
Chicago campus), a tenant will pay more for less. Broadly, Evanston tends to be
more expensive than the north side of Chicago, but things also get more
expensive the closer you get to downtown Chicago. Most students live in Evanston
or the north side of Chicago (areas like Rogers Park and Edgewater are popular).

Depending on what type of transportation options you have, finding a place near
public transport might also be a consideration. Fortunately the CTA is pretty
extensive and the red/purple lines can take you directly to/from campus from
most places on the north side of Chicago. In Evanston and near Loyola university
(in Rogers Park and Edgewater) there is also the Intercampus shuttle.
Northwestern Off-Campus Life has more information about [transportation in the
area](http://www.northwestern.edu/offcampus/resources/getting-around/index.html).

If you're looking for roommates, you can connect with other Northwestern
students through the [Off-Campus Housing
website](https://www.northwestern.edu/offcampus/housing/find-housing.html).
