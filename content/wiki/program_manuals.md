Please refer to your program's manual for information about expectations, requirements, and resources. The version of the manual which applies to you depends on your date of admission. For example, a new manual released in Fall 2025 would not apply (by default) to students admitted before Fall 2025. However, students can choose opt-in to newer editions of the manual.

> **The archive of old Ph.D manuals is incomplete.** Contributions are welcome and woulve be greatly appreciated!
